SAP Labs Network are SAP’s core research and development entities, developing and constantly improving key SAP solutions. These locations are strategically located in high-tech clusters around the globe and reflect our culture of diversity and innovation. Each lab has a particular focus on specific applications, technology, or the market. With 20 labs in 17 countries, the SAP Labs Network drives thought leadership globally and in local ecosystems, allowing SAP to innovate, grow, and succeed.Being plugged into the local ecosystem is a true competitive advantage so each lab seeks out partners, universities, and startups to build relationships as a trusted peer and thought leader on topics of strategic importance to SAP. For example, while labs in Paris and Canada focus on analytics applications, Latin America localizes SAP solutions for the world’s markets and Bulgaria refines the technology around SAP’s  Business Technology Platform for real-time computing.
